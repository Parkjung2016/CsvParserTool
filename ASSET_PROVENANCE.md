# Asset Provenance and AI Disclosure

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

## Theme artwork

`assets/themes/grassland.png` and `assets/themes/ocean.png` were generated from
text prompts using OpenAI image-generation tools. No third-party image file was
intentionally copied or embedded in the prompts or final assets.

## Mascot artwork

The four files under `assets/mascot/` were generated using OpenAI
image-generation tools. The generated images were locally processed to remove
their flat background, crop transparent margins, and produce application-ready
PNG files.

## Application icon

`assets/pjdev-icon-source.png`, `assets/pjdev-icon-source-square.png`, and
`Properties/AppIcon.ico` are resized or reformatted derivatives of
`assets/mascot/mascot-hello.png`. They replace earlier icon artwork whose
provenance could not be verified.

## Important note

AI-generated output may not be unique and the copyright status of AI-generated
material can vary by jurisdiction. The project identifies AI-generated assets
transparently and licenses any rights held by the project author under
`ASSET_LICENSE.md`.
